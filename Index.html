Information about how to use Student Corner.

How to use the Forum?

Depending on what subject you need help with, you will choose the person who is assigned to that specific subject and ask them about it by messaging them in the forum page of the website.

What Subjects are included?

Student Corner provides a forum where learners can communicate with our students to help them with their studies in Mathematics, Science, English, Filipino, Research, Religions, Entrepreneurship, UCSP, MIL, ICT, CPAR, and PE.

Who are the ones helping in the forum?

The people helping you in the forum are graduating senior high students who are here to help you and other learners with your studies.
