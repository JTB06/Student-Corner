About us

We are an assistive learning website

Our website is an interactive platform wherein our personnel helps students who are requesting for assistance with their studies. Each of Student Corner's personnel is assigned to specific subjects that they will mainly focus on with helping the learners. This website acts as a medium for the direct communication of the team behind Student Corner and the learners who are in need of our tutoring or assisting services.
